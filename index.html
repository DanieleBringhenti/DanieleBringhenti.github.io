About

I am Daniele Bringhenti, a Ph.D. Student at the Politecnico di Torino. I have been pursuing the Ph.D title since 2019, within the Computer Network Group (NetGroup), in the Department of Control and Computer Engineering (DAUIN). In this educational path, I have been researching network security automation, under the supervision of Professor Riccardo Sisto, leader of the NetGroup, and Fulvio Valenza.

My main research interests are:

  • innovative networking technologies ad paradigms (e.g., NFV, SDN);
  • automatic orchestration and configuration of security functions in virtualized networks;
  • formal verification of network security policies.
